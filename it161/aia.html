AIA (Audience, Issues, and Approach)

Client's name and final decision maker for the website: Vonnie Gaither
Relationship: Family friend
Client's business name: Art by Vonnie
Redesign of: Art by Vonnie
Type of website: Identity, informational, and marketing. This will be a portfolio website that showcases a gallery of her various paintings and artwork as well as provide contact information.
Client's goals: My client's goal for this website is to maintain an online presence and overall relevance of her abilities as a contemporary painter in the Anchorage art community. It will also serve to provide a proper online platform where her artwork can be easily viewed and shared by others.
Audience Issues Approach
Art Gallery Curators:
This group is in search of potential artwork to add to a collection at a gallery. Curators will likely be middle-aged and middle- to upper-class with an equal distribution of genders. They will have intermediate to advanced computer and internet skills, use both Macs and PCs as well as mobile devices like smart phones and tables, and prefer Safari, Chrome, or Firefox.
  • View available artwork
  • View history of collections and projects
  • View artist profile and style
  • Contact information for Vonnie
  • Provide photos of artist's work in easily-navigable, chronologically-ordered collections
  • Provide informational page and links to current galleries
  • Provide informational page of current projects
  • Contact information page with phone number, email address, and contact form
  • About Me page with artist's professional profile and art style
Prospective Buyers:
This group will be using the website to view artwork that they would be interested in purchasing. Buyers will likely be at least middle-class with disposable income and adults ages 25 and older. They will use a variety of devices to access the site including desktops and mobile devices. Browsers and operating systems will also vary. They will be at least somewhat technically-savvy, enough to navigate websites and make online purchases.
  • View available artwork
  • View artwork pricing
  • Contact information for Vonnie
  • Provide for-sale page with photos of available artwork and pricing
  • Shopping cart
Fellow Artists
Other artists and painters in the local community interested in collaborating with Vonnie will be able to connect with her through the website. They will range in age from young adults to middle-aged and older and will be mid to high income individuals. They will likely use mobile devices but there will be desktop users as well.
  • View artwork
  • View information on past and current projects
  • Review artist profile and style
  • Contact information for Vonnie
  • Provide photos of paintings in easily-navigable, chronologically-ordered collections
  • Informational page on current projects
  • Provide links to current galleries
  • Contact information page with phone number, email address, and contact form
